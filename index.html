  • About Me

    I am currently a PhD Candidate at the School of Computing, Clemson University. My advisor, Dr. Eric Patterson, is Associate Professor, Associate Director of Digital Production Arts at the School of Computing, Clemson University. My current research interests are computer graphics about rendering, modeling, facial recognition, and construction. I got my computer science Master's degree from Oregon State University.

    C/C++, and Python are my primary computer languages. I am developing my skill in OpenGL, Qt, GLSL, and OpenCV since I work in computer graphics. I became a graduate teaching assistant in 2017. On the April 10th 2020, I won the school of computing outstanding teaching assitant award.
